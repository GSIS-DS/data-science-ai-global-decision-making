# Global Data Sources

Key sources for international economic, policy, and development data.

## Major Providers

- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) - Development indicators, global statistics
- [IMF Data](https://data.imf.org/) - International financial statistics
- [OECD Data](https://data.oecd.org/) - OECD countries and regional comparisons
- [UN Comtrade](https://comtrade.un.org/) - International trade data
- [WTO Statistics](https://stats.wto.org/) - Trade and economics
- [UNData](https://data.un.org/) - United Nations statistical database
- [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/) - Global development visualization
- [FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/) - Economic time series
- [National Statistical Offices](#) - Country-specific data
- [Central Banks](#) - Monetary and financial data

## Note

For this course, we document sources and provide placeholders. We do not download large datasets; assignments guide you through accessing what you need.
