# Capstone Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (A) | Satisfactory (B-C) | Needs Work (D-F) |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Question Quality | Clearly defined, relevant to track, answerable with data | Somewhat clear but may be broad | Unclear or unanswerable |
| Data Quality | Multiple sources, well-documented, thoughtful cleaning | Adequate sources, some documentation | Limited sources, poor documentation |
| Analytical Appropriateness | Methods match questions, appropriate complexity | Basic methods applied | Inappropriate methods or errors |
| Reproducibility | Complete notebook, clear steps, runs without errors | Mostly complete, minor issues | Incomplete or broken |
| Interpretation | Thoughtful limitations, clear claims, no false causality | Some limitations acknowledged | Claims exceed evidence |
| Visualization | Clear, labeled, ethical design, supports story | Some design issues | Poor or misleading |
| Responsible AI | Full disclosure, verification shown | Partial disclosure | Undeclared or unchecked use |
| Teamwork/Presentation | Professional, clear, engaging, well-prepared | Some issues | Disorganized or incomplete |
