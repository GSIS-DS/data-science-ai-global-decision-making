# Capstone Rubric

The team project is worth 25% of the course grade. The individual defense or reflection is a separate 5% component.

## Team Project Rubric

| Category | Weight | Excellent | Competent | Developing | Insufficient evidence |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Question quality and decision relevance | 10% | Focused, answerable, and tied to a specific decision and audience | Clear question with plausible decision relevance | Broad or weakly connected to a decision | Unclear, unanswerable, or missing |
| Data quality and documentation | 15% | Credible sources, complete dictionary, traceable cleaning, and explicit constraints | Sources and major transformations documented | Important definitions or transformations are incomplete | Source, provenance, or cleaning cannot be evaluated |
| Analytical appropriateness | 15% | Methods fit the question and data, with justified choices and useful checks | Methods are generally appropriate and correctly used | Some choices are unjustified or technically fragile | Methods do not support the stated question |
| Reproducibility | 10% | A new reviewer can run the workflow from documented instructions | Main workflow runs with minor clarification | Significant manual steps or fragile paths remain | Core results cannot be reproduced |
| Interpretation and limitations | 15% | Claims match evidence and address uncertainty, alternatives, and limits | Conclusions are supported and key limits noted | Some overclaiming or superficial limitations | Conclusions conflict with or exceed the evidence |
| Visualization and communication | 15% | Accessible, accurate, decision-ready visuals and concise memo | Clear outputs with appropriate labels and narrative | Design or narrative sometimes obscures the result | Outputs are misleading, unreadable, or incomplete |
| Responsible AI use and verification | 10% | Use is fully disclosed, checked, and governed by a clear human decision | Material use and primary checks are documented | Disclosure or verification is incomplete | Undeclared use or unverified substantive output |
| Teamwork and professional presentation | 10% | Responsibilities, integration, and presentation are consistently professional | Team produces a coherent submission and presentation | Uneven coordination affects some deliverables | Serious coordination problems leave work incomplete |

## Individual Defense or Reflection Rubric

| Category | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Explains personal contribution with evidence | 30% |
| Explains key data and analytical choices | 30% |
| Identifies limitations and responsible AI decisions | 25% |
| Responds clearly and professionally | 15% |

Performance descriptors are applied using the same four levels: excellent, competent, developing, and insufficient evidence.
