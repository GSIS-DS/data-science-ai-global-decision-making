# AI Verification Example

## Task

Ask an AI to calculate average GDP growth by region.

## Prompt Used

"Calculate average GDP growth by region from the global indicators dataset and explain what it shows."

## Output Received

AI returned: "Asia has the highest average GDP growth at 5.2%, followed by South America at 3.1%..."

## Verification

- Checked against actual data: Asia average is 5.18% (correct)
- Verified all regions included
- Confirmed calculation method

## Changes Made

- Clarified that 2020-2022 average was requested
- Added standard deviation for context

## Limitations

- Sample dataset is synthetic
- Time period limited to 3 years
